# StudyAI - Final Premium Design System

## Typography Excellence
- **Poppins** - Modern, clean sans-serif for body text (300-900 weights)
- **Cormorant Garamond** - Elegant serif for headings and titles
- **Fira Code** - Professional monospace for code/data

## Color Palette - Modern Premium
- **Background**: #09111d (Deep navy, almost black with slight blue tone)
- **Card**: #0f1829 (Slightly lighter navy for depth)
- **Accent**: #d4a574 (Sophisticated warm taupe/gold)
- **Text**: #f8fafc (Clean off-white, easier on eyes than pure white)
- **Borders**: #1a2940 (Subtle, defined but not harsh)

### Why This Palette Works
- **Sophisticated**: Warm taupe replaces harsh yellow/gold
- **Modern**: Deep blues feel contemporary, not generic
- **Professional**: Works for enterprise and education
- **Balanced**: Colors have proper contrast without being harsh
- **Premium**: Feels like a luxury product, not AI-generated

## Enhanced Animations
- `slideUp/slideDown/slideIn` - Smooth 0.6s entrances with 24px movement
- `scaleIn` - Premium easing (0.95 scale to full)
- `glow` - Pulsing ambient light effect
- `float` - Subtle hovering motion (8px vertical)
- `gradient` - Flowing background animations
- `shimmer` - Premium loading states

## Visual Enhancements
- **Glass Morphism**: backdrop-blur-2xl on nav and overlays
- **Gradient Logos**: Subtle animated backgrounds
- **Layered Shadows**: Multiple shadow layers for depth
- **Smooth Transitions**: cubic-bezier easing for polish
- **Typography Scale**: Proper h1-h6 sizing with tracking adjustments
